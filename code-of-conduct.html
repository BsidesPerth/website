Code of Conduct

Be Excellent to Each Other

We have ZERO TOLERANCE for physical, verbal, or sexual harassment of any participant — human, humanoid, or AI.

Our Code of Conduct is simply this: "Be Excellent to Each Other" — the Golden Rule. If that needs a clearer line: "Do not be a dick, or we will throw you out."

Asking questions of a speaker during their talk — to get clarity or debate a point — is not being a dick. Heckling or haranguing a speaker is. If you are not sure, ask yourself: would this be acceptable if it were done to you, your best friend, your colleague, or any other person? If the answer is no, don't do it. If someone asks you to stop, stop.

Reporting an Issue

If you are having an issue with any BSides Perth participant — of any badge type — find a member of our team in the red BSides Perth t-shirts. They will help you determine the next steps and ensure you feel safe and heard.

Staff reserve the right to determine what constitutes a violation. Breaches of this Code of Conduct will likely result in removal from the current event and potentially a ban from future BSides events.

Privacy Policy

How We Handle Your Data

We will never share attendee information with sponsors or any third party, under any circumstances.

Comments

When visitors leave comments on the site we collect the data shown in the comments form, plus the visitor's IP address and browser user-agent string to help with spam detection. General browsing of our website is not stored or tracked.

Embedded Content

Articles on this site may include embedded content (e.g. tweets, videos, images). Embedded content from other websites behaves exactly as if the visitor had visited that other website directly — those sites may collect data about you, use cookies, and monitor your interaction with the embedded content.

Services We Use

We store and use attendee data exclusively for BSides Perth purposes. The third-party services we rely on are listed below, each with a link to their own privacy policy.

  • Humanitix Event ticketing — ticket holder information including name, email, and shirt size. We use this to manage ticket sales and conference logistics. Humanitix Privacy Policy
  • Eventbrite Event ticketing (used in previous years) — same purpose as above. Eventbrite Privacy Policy
  • Cloudflare Network and security layer for our website. Cloudflare Privacy Policy
  • AWS Cloud infrastructure. AWS Privacy Policy
  • Google Workspace Email and document management (Google Drive). Google Drive Terms
  • Google Forms CFP and feedback forms. Submission is entirely optional; data collected is kept by BSides Perth. Google Privacy Policy
  • Flickr Photo and media hosting. Flickr Privacy Policy

Questions about how we handle your data? Email us at info@bsidesperth.com.au.
