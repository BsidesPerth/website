Why attend?

BSides Perth brings together security practitioners, researchers, students, and enthusiasts for two days of genuine knowledge sharing. Every dollar of sponsorship and ticket revenue is invested directly back into the event — into the venue, swag, food, and the community that makes it worthwhile.

Expect high-quality technical talks, hands-on workshops, a challenging CTF, and the chance to meet the people building and defending Australian security infrastructure.

Students and those from under-represented backgrounds are especially welcome. If cost is a barrier, reach out — we have options.

About

Built By the Community, For the Community

Security BSides is a global series of community-driven information security conferences. Inspired by the BSides Manifesto, BSides Perth was founded to bring that same spirit of openness, accessibility, and genuine knowledge-sharing to Western Australia.

Since our inaugural event in 2017, we've hosted hundreds of speakers, thousands of attendees, and no shortage of memorable moments — all made possible by sponsors, volunteers, and a community that keeps showing up.

Diverse, inclusive & safe

We actively work to make BSides Perth welcoming to everyone — regardless of background, experience level, or career stage.

Every dollar goes back to participants

All sponsorship income is reinvested into the event — the venue, food, swag, and experience. We run at cost.

Tickets stay affordable

We work hard to minimise ticket costs, with concession options available for students and those who need them.
